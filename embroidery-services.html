Our Embroidery Services

At Kunozulkhair Tailoring Shop, our embroidery services turn ordinary garments into personalized treasures with custom designs, monograms, and decorative elements.

With state-of-the-art equipment and skilled artisans, we create beautiful embroidery for clothing, accessories, home textiles, and special occasion items that reflect your personal style.

Whether you need simple monogramming for a corporate uniform or intricate designs for traditional attire, our embroidery services add that special touch that makes your items uniquely yours.

Quality Craftsmanship

Custom Designs

Frequently Asked Questions

We can embroider a wide variety of items including clothing (shirts, jackets, caps, etc.), accessories (bags, towels), home textiles (pillows, tablecloths), and more. Most fabric types can be embroidered, though some materials work better than others.

Absolutely! You can provide your own design, logo, or artwork, and we'll convert it into an embroidery-ready format. For best results, vector files (AI, EPS) or high-resolution images are preferred. We can also help modify designs to work better for embroidery.

Turnaround time depends on the complexity of the design and our current workload. Simple designs on a single item can often be completed in 1-2 business days. Larger orders typically take 3-7 business days. Rush services are available for an additional fee.

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders. Pricing is tiered based on quantity, with significant savings available for larger orders. Please contact us for a custom quote for your specific requirements.

To maintain the quality of embroidery, we recommend washing embroidered items inside-out in cold water on a gentle cycle. Avoid bleach and harsh detergents. Air drying is preferred, but if you use a dryer, use low heat. Iron on the reverse side of the embroidery when needed.
